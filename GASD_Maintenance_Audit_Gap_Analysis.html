Odoo Transaction Flow — Current State vs. Required State

Red boxes indicate missing or non-compliant Odoo steps that must be implemented. Based on S-001 through S-022 findings and observed ticket behaviour.

Current Odoo Workflow — Observed (with gaps highlighted)
1
⚠ Ticket Creation — INCOMPLETE
Client name, location, issue type entered. Missing: scheduling preference, SLA tier, project linkage, priority classification, warranty verification flag. No mandatory field enforcement.
2
⚠ Assignment — NO COMPETENCE CHECK
Ticket assigned to available team without competence or leave check. No In-Charge approval step. No materials pre-check triggering. SLA clock starts at assignment, not at confirmed arrival.
3
⚠ Dispatch — NO CONFIRMATION GATE
Team dispatched without Odoo capturing client confirmation. No blocking gate. Dispatch possible at any hour. No pre-work checklist linked to task type.
4
⚠ On-Site Execution — EVIDENCE-FREE
No pre-work photo upload step. No site protection confirmation. No client consent capture. Teams update Odoo only at close-out if at all. No SQC (Site Quality Check) step exists in Odoo workflow.
5
⚠ Material Replacement — NO JUSTIFICATION FIELD
Materials replaced without Odoo field for technical justification. No In-Charge sign-off required for replacements above cost threshold. Same individuals raise and approve material requests.
6
⚠ Ticket Close-Out — NO EVIDENCE GATE
Ticket closed without: customer signature, pre/post photos, SQC confirmation, or sub-ticket for unresolved issues. Cancellations possible without justification or reviewer sign-off. MAN14624 cancelled suppressing a complaint.
Required Odoo Workflow — Target State (Post-Remediation)
1
Ticket Creation — Mandatory Fields
Required: client name, location, contact, issue description, photos/video, scheduling preference, SLA tier (Urgent/Medium/Low), project linkage, warranty eligibility flag (30-day check). Cannot submit without all fields.
2
Assignment — Competence & Leave Gate
Team assignment validated against competency matrix and leave calendar. Staff with active performance flags require In-Charge co-approval. Pre-departure materials checklist auto-generated from task type. Leave-aware auto-reassignment if assignee unavailable.
3
Client Confirmation Gate
Coordinator must confirm client availability and log confirmation in Odoo before dispatch is permitted. No-dispatch-without-confirmation rule enforced. SLA clock starts at confirmed appointment, not at task assignment. Dispatch time restricted to defined working hours unless In-Charge authorises after-hours.
4
On-Site Execution — Evidence Checkpoints
Step 4a: Pre-work photos (min 3) uploaded before work can begin. Step 4b: Site protection confirmation checkbox. Step 4c: Client consent acknowledgement (scope explained). Step 4d: Technical task execution per SOP. Step 4e: SQC — additional defects logged as sub-tickets. Material replacements require technical justification field + In-Charge sign-off above threshold.
5
Materials & Escalation Flow
Materials shortage triggers mandatory Coordinator notification sub-task in Odoo. Follow-up sub-ticket auto-created. Client receives confirmed completion date within 24 hours. Out-of-scope requests generate referral to Sales or Installation department with referral reference number.
6
Ticket Close-Out — Evidence Gate
Cannot close without: post-work photos, customer acknowledgement (digital signature / SMS confirmation / written sign-off), SQC confirmation, and resolution of all sub-tickets or escalation trail. Cancellation requires: documented justification, independent reviewer sign-off, min 3 logged contact attempts. SLA performance auto-reported to In-Charge dashboard monthly.
